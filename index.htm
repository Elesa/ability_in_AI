{Dis}Ability in AI is a newly formed group that aims at supporting and advocating for disabled people.
Our group is an active member of NeurIPS , ICML and ICLR.

If you are interested in becoming a member of {Dis}Ability in AI or organizer of our future events, please email disability@nips.cc
with a statement of your interest in {Dis}Ability in AI and a description of your experience with AI/ML and the disabilities community.
Our Vision:
Equal participation for all

What do we mean by the term disabled people :
The term disabled people is used to include all those who experience barriers in accessing education due to having or being considered to have an impairment. This includes
  • people with physical or sensory impairments
  • people with specific learning difficulties (such as dyslexia, dyspraxia or AD(H)D)
  • people with mental health conditions (such as anxiety and depression)
  • people with autism spectrum conditions


Action points:

  1. advocate for disabled people;
  2. host mentoring sessions during conferences/workshops;
  3. escort attendees that require assistance to the front row of any of the sessions or workshops;
  4. have volunteers to assist during poster sessions - to get them closer to the poster or to provide a chair if needed for the presenter, etc;
